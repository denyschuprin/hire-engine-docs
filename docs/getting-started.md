# Getting Started

## Signing in

1. Open the Hire Engine web address in your browser.
2. Enter your **email** and **password**.
3. Click **Sign in**.

You'll land on the **Companies** list, your home base for everything.

!!! warning "Can't sign in?"
    If you see *"Invalid email or password"*, double-check both fields. If you
    don't have an account yet, an administrator needs to create one for you
    (see [Managing Users](admin/users.md)).

## Finding your way around

Once you're in, the screen has two parts:

- **The sidebar on the left** - your main menu. It always shows:
    - **Companies** - the list of every company you're working on.
    - **Users** - only visible if you're an administrator.
    - **Logout** - at the bottom, to sign out securely.
- **The main area on the right** - whatever you're currently working on.

## How work flows

Every company moves through a series of **stages**, shown as a colored badge
and a progress strip on the company's page:

| Stage | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Just added. Information gathering is about to begin. |
| **Enriching** | Hire Engine is reading the company's websites right now. |
| **Enriched** | Information collected - ready to create the profile. |
| **Generating** | The profile text is being drafted. |
| **Generated** | Draft ready - time to review, edit and approve. |
| **Publishing** | The profile is being sent to Kununu. |
| **Published** | The profile draft is saved on Kununu. |
| **Failed** | Something didn't finish - you can retry it. |

!!! tip
    Badges that are **pulsing** mean work is happening in the background. The
    page refreshes the status on its own - no need to reload.

## A quick word on "background" work

Several actions - collecting information, drafting the profile, publishing -
take anywhere from a few seconds to a couple of minutes. Hire Engine handles
these **in the background**: you can move to another screen and come back, and
the status will have updated on its own.
