# Adding a Company

Everything in Hire Engine starts with a company. Adding one is quick - and as
soon as you do, the system begins gathering information automatically.

## The Companies list

When you sign in, you're on the **Companies** list. From here you can:

- **Search** for a company by name.
- **Filter** by stage using the row of buttons (All, Pending, Enriching,
  Enriched, and so on).
- **Click any row** to open that company.
- **Add a new company** with the button at the top right.

Each row shows the company name, website, current stage, country, how many
information sources are attached, and when it was added.

## Add a new company

1. On the Companies list, click **Add Company** (top right).
2. Fill in the form:

    | Field | Required? | Notes |
    |---|---|---|
    | **Company Name** | Yes | At least 2 characters, e.g. *Acme GmbH*. |
    | **Website URL** | Yes | Must start with `https://`. |
    | **Career URL** | Optional | The company's jobs/careers page. Add it now if you have it - it's needed later for collecting jobs. Must start with `https://`. |
    | **Wikipedia URL** | Optional | An English or German Wikipedia article, if one exists. |
    | **Country** | Yes | Choose from the list. |
    | **Industry** | Optional | E.g. *Enterprise Software*. |

3. Click **Add Company**.

You'll be taken straight to the new company's page, and Hire Engine immediately
begins **collecting information** in the background.

!!! tip "Add the Career URL early"
    The Career URL isn't required to create the company, but you'll need it to
    collect and publish **jobs** later. Adding it now saves a step. You can
    always add or change it afterwards.

!!! warning "Duplicate websites"
    If a company with the same website already exists, you'll see *"A company
    with this website URL already exists."* Open the existing one instead of
    creating a duplicate.

## The company page

A company's page is the hub for all of its work. At the top you'll see:

- The company **logo** (once one has been found) and **name**.
- The current **stage badge**.
- The **website**, **country** and **industry**.
- Action buttons: **Edit**, **Enrich Now**, **Generate Profile**, and **Delete**.

Below that is a **progress strip** showing how far along the company is, and a
row of **tabs** for each area of work:

> **Sources · Generated Content · Kununu Profile · Career Page · Credentials · Jobs**

The rest of this guide walks through these tabs in order.

## Editing or deleting a company

- **Edit** - click **Edit** at the top to change the name, web addresses,
  country or industry, then **Save**.
- **Delete** - click **Delete**, then confirm.

!!! danger "Delete is permanent"
    Deleting a company permanently removes it **and** all of its sources,
    profile content, jobs and saved credentials. This cannot be undone.
