# Your Career Page

Every company gets a ready-made **public career page** - a polished page showing
the approved profile and the company's open jobs. You can share its link or
embed it directly on the company's own website. This is set up on the
**Career Page** tab.

## What the career page shows

Once the profile is **approved**, the page goes live and displays:

- A branded header with the company **logo**, **name** and **tagline**.
- The full profile: who they are, products & services, benefits, what makes them
  special, photos, videos, employee testimonials, locations and application tips.
- **Open positions** - every published job, each with an **Apply** button
  (this section stays empty until you publish jobs).
- Social media links.

!!! note "Before approval"
    Until the profile is approved, visitors see a friendly *"coming soon"*
    message instead of the content.

## The web addresses

On the **Career Page** tab you can choose how people reach the page:

- **Default address** - always works, no setup needed:
  `…/careers/{company id}`. Use the **Open** link to view it.
- **Custom slug** *(optional)* - a friendlier ending, e.g. `…/careers/acme-gmbh`.
  Use lowercase letters, numbers and hyphens only.
- **Custom domain** *(optional)* - the company's own address, e.g.
  `karriere.acme.de`.

Click **Save URL Config** to apply your choices.

!!! info "Using a custom domain"
    A custom domain needs a small DNS change first: add a **CNAME record**
    pointing the domain to the hosting, then enter the domain here. Your
    technical contact can set up the CNAME.

## Preview the live page

Click **View Career Page** (available once the profile is approved) to open the
public page in a new tab exactly as visitors will see it.

## Embed it on the company's website

Prefer the page to live inside the company's existing site (for example at
`/karriere`)?

1. On the **Career Page** tab, expand **Embed on your company website**.
2. Click **Copy** to copy the embed snippet.
3. Paste it into any page of the company's website.

The career page will appear right inside that page, and it stays up to date
automatically whenever you publish changes.
