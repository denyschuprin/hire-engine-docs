# Creating the Profile

This is the heart of Hire Engine: turning the collected information into a
complete, polished **employer profile** in German. The system writes the first
draft; you review, edit and approve it.

You'll find this on the **Generated Content** tab of a company.

## Generate the first draft

1. Make sure the company is **Enriched** (at least one source finished).
2. On the **Generated Content** tab, click **Generate Profile**.
3. The status changes to **Generating** - this runs in the background and
   usually takes a minute or two. The page updates on its own when it's done.

When finished, you'll see the full draft, organized into sections.

!!! note "Where the text comes from"
    The draft is written from everything that was collected - the website,
    Wikipedia, careers page and more - and crafted into ready-to-use German
    employer-branding copy.

## The profile sections

The profile is made of clearly labelled sections (in German), including:

- **Header / Tagline** - a short, catchy intro line
- **Wer wir sind** - who we are
- **Produkte, Services & Leistungen** - products and services
- **Perspektiven für die Zukunft** - future outlook
- **Was uns besonders macht** - what makes us special
- **Aufgabengebiete** - areas of work
- **Gesuchte Qualifikationen** - desired qualifications
- **Bewerber-Intro** and **Erwartungen an Bewerber** - applicant intro & expectations
- **Bewerbungstipps**, **Bevorzugte Bewerbungsform**, **Auswahlverfahren** - application tips, preferred format, selection process
- **Standorte Inland / Ausland** - domestic & international locations
- **Benefits** - a list of perk labels plus a free-text description

Below the text sections you'll also find **Photos**, **Videos**,
**Testimonials** and **Social Media links** (covered further down).

## Editing the text

Each section sits in a card you can expand or collapse.

1. Click a section to expand it.
2. Click the **pencil icon** to edit.
3. Make your changes:
    - **Text sections** open a simple editor - you can make text **bold** or
      *italic* and adjust the wording.
    - **Tagline** is a single line.
    - **Benefits labels** are entered separated by commas; each becomes a tidy
      green tag.
4. Click **Save** (*Speichern*) to keep your change, or **Cancel**
   (*Abbrechen*) to discard it.

!!! tip "Your edits are protected"
    The moment you edit a section, Hire Engine **locks** it automatically. If
    you later regenerate the profile, your locked sections are kept exactly as
    you wrote them.

## Locking and unlocking sections

A **lock** decides whether the system may rewrite a section the next time you
regenerate.

- **Locked** (filled lock icon) - your wording is protected; regeneration won't
  touch it.
- **Unlocked** (outline lock icon) - regeneration may rewrite it.

Click the lock icon on any section to toggle it. You can also use **Lock All**
or **Unlock All** at the top to do every section at once.

## Photos, videos, testimonials and social links

Below the text, you can curate the media that appears on the profile and career
page:

- **Photos (Einblicke)** - pick which images to show. Move pictures between
  **Selected** and the **Pool** of available images, then **Save**.
- **Videos** - add a YouTube, Vimeo or TikTok link, and remove ones you don't
  want.
- **Testimonials** - add employee quotes. For each, fill in the **name**,
  **department**, an optional **photo** and the **quote**, then **Save**. Use
  **+ Add testimonial** for more.
- **Social Media** - fill in the company's LinkedIn, XING, Facebook, Instagram,
  YouTube, Twitter/X and TikTok addresses. Leave a field empty to omit it.

Each of these can also be **locked** so regeneration won't change your choices.

## Approve the profile

When the profile reads well and the media is set:

1. Click **Approve** at the top of the **Generated Content** tab.
2. The profile is marked **Approved** - it records who approved it and when.

Approval is what makes the profile eligible to **publish to Kununu** and to
appear on the **public career page**.

## Regenerate (a fresh draft)

Want the system to take another pass? Click **Regenerate**. This creates a new
**version** of the profile while keeping every **locked** section exactly as it
is. Unlocked sections get fresh wording.

## Versions

Switch to the **Versions** view to see the history - each version's number,
whether it was approved, who approved it, and when. This lets you track how the
profile has evolved.

## Export

Need the text outside the platform - for a review or a quick backup? Use
**Export** to download the profile as a plain text file, one section after
another.
